    How to use

    📝 Tasks

    • Add — Click + Add Task to open the task form. Fill in title, description, due date, priority, project, repeat frequency, and tags.
    • Complete — Check the checkbox
    • Edit — Click ✏ to open the task form and edit all fields
    • Delete — Click ✕
    • Reorder — Drag and drop tasks

    🏷️ Tags

    Add tags in the task form (no # needed). Each tag gets its own color automatically. Click a tag pill to filter tasks by that tag.

    📅 Due Dates & Repeat

    Set a due date and optional repeat frequency (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Yearly). Repeating tasks are automatically recreated on their next due date when completed.

    🎨 Themes

    Click 🌙/☀️ to toggle between dark and light mode.

    ⏱️ Pomodoro Timer

    Use the timer for focus sessions (25 min focus / 5 min break). Click Pin to open a small always-on-top window that stays visible even when you switch tabs.
